Timeline

Here you can see our placements from different games in different leagues from around the last 3 years.
They are ordered chronologically, and are colour coded per game.

  • NUEL Summer Finals 2018

    CS:GO - August 20 2018

    The Warwick Stretchyboiz competed live for a best of 3 against the Lancaster Bombers. They won 2-0, keeping their title as the best university CS:GO team in the UK, as well as walking away with some well deserved prizes.

  • NSEI 2018

    Overwatch - May 2 2018

    When invited down to the Redbull Gaming Sphere to play against UCL, we brought our best. In the first competitive tournament of the day Warwick Esports won with a 3-0 victory. Each player was gifted £15 Blizzard credit as a reward.

  • NUEL Spring Finals 2018

    CS:GO - April 28 2018

    The Warwick Stretchyboiz travelled down to London for a live best of 5 against the Lancaster Bombers. They walked away with a 3-1 victory, a ROG keyboard, tickets to insomnia later that year, and ECS VIP finals tickets.

  • NUEL Community Tournament

    PUBG - April 12 2018

    For the first time in history the NUEL were able to get their hands on a PUBG server, Warwick Esports submitted 2 teams for this tournament. Players of the Warwick Stretchyboiz and JNormalchild were able to take the victory.

  • Arrow League Season 1

    Rocket League - March 20 2018

    The Warwick A team competed in the first Arrow League Rocket League tournament and walked away champions after winning the final series.

  • King of the North 2018

    CS:GO - March 18 2018

    Warwick Stretchyboiz travelled up to Media City Salford to face off as underdogs against the Lancaster Bombers. They were able to pull off an impressive 2-0 win, and walk away with £200 in prize money.

  • USL Season 1 [2nd Place]

    R6S - December 12 2017

    The Warwick Castles came second in the founding University Siege League season, making them the top team where all players were from the same university.

  • FragSoc 2017 [2nd Place]

    CS:GO - November 25 2017

    Warwick Esports sent the Stretchyboiz to York for a weekend of CS:GO, managing to bring back 2nd place after losing to the Lancaster Bombers; although securing more rounds overall.

  • UEM 2017 [2nd Place]

    LoL - July 28 2017

    Grey Warwick were flown out to take part in the UEMasters (following their NUEL finals win), and made it all the way to finals before unfortunately falling to the Portuguese university team.

  • NUEL Live 2017

    LoL - March 25 2017

    Hosted at Birmingham University, Grey Warwick made their way to NUEL live to compete in the finals against Aston Yellowtails. They won a very close 3-0 in the best of 5 series, walking away with all expenses paid trip to the EULCS finals in Germany that year!
