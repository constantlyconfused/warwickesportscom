About us

Who we are

We are a student run organisation based in the University of Warwick, and supported by the Warwick SU. Established in mid 2017 to unite all gaming societies at the university, we shortly became the most dominant esports society regularly placing 1st and 2nd in many competitive events.

What we do

As well as facilitating students' participation in various UK esports leagues, we provide a platform for players to promote themselves as individuals and in teams. We also run termly events such as in-house tournaments and LAN parties.

Games

We have a competitive team for almost every popular game right now, head over to the games page to find out more about each community and how you can get involved. We're always looking for new talent and friendly players to join the community.

All things wolf

We aim to raise the bar nationally and internationally when it comes to student esports. Not only in the standard of our teams and players, but also the professionalism of student run organisations.
Working closely with league organisations such as the National University Esports League and National Student Esports we hope to promote and legitimise collegiate esports in the industry.

Contact our team

We always love to hear from industry figures, students, alumni and organisations about opportunities and promotions we can offer our members.
